Code of Conduct

We strive to make this conference a welcoming and inclusive space for everyone. Please use your real name when accessing our systems. Harassment of any kind will not be tolerated, regardless of gender, gender identity or expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion (or lack thereof), or scientific opinion.

All communication must be appropriate for a professional and diverse audience. Sexual language and imagery are not permitted in any part of the conference, whether in-person or online.

If you experience or witness harassment, please contact the conference staff immediately. A dedicated safety and inclusion team will review all reports and may take appropriate action, including warnings or expulsion from the event.

Harassment includes (but is not limited to):

  • Offensive comments related to gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, age, religion, or technology choices
  • Sexual language and images in any conference platform
  • Intimidation, stalking, or unwelcome following
  • Non-consensual photography or recording
  • Sustained disruption of talks or events
  • Inappropriate sexual attention
  • Advocating for or encouraging any of the above behaviors
