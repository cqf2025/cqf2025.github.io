XIV Conference on Quantum Foundations

From Foundations to Quantum Computing

Celebrating IYQ2025

Buenos Aires, Argentina, November 26-28

The XIV Conference on Quantum Foundations: From Foundations to Quantum Computing - Celebrating IYQ2025 will take place from November 26th to 28th at the Instituto de Ciencias de la Computación in Buenos Aires, Argentina. This edition of the CQF coincides with the International Year of Quantum Science and Technology (IYQ 2025), as proclaimed by the United Nations General Assembly, recognizing the importance of quantum science in shaping future technologies.

The conference will bring together leading experts to explore various aspects of quantum foundations that have a significant impact on quantum technologies, featuring a distinguished lineup of invited speakers.

As is tradition in CQF meetings, the event will foster discussions among researchers, postdoctoral fellows, and graduate students, promoting an engaging and collaborative exchange on the fundamental questions of quantum theory and its applications in a friendly and stimulating environment.

Registration is required for in-person attendance. There is no registration fee.

For inquiries, please contact fundamentoscuantica@gmail.com.
